George Brown College is a renowned institution located in downtown Toronto, offering a wide range of career-focused programs and practical learning opportunities. 
It emphasizes industry partnerships and hands-on training to ensure students gain the skills needed for successful careers.

- Established in 1967: George Brown College has been serving students for over 50 years.
- Diverse Programs: The college offers more than 160 programs across various fields such as business, health, technology, and arts.
- Hands-On Learning: Students gain practical experience through internships, co-op placements, and collaborations with local businesses.
- International Community: George Brown College has a diverse student body, with many international students from around the world.
