The COMP 3104 DevOps course equips students with the essential skills to implement DevOps practices and tools in modern software development and IT operations.
It focuses on automation, continuous integration, and continuous delivery to optimize collaboration between development and operations teams.

- Principles of DevOps: Learn the core concepts of DevOps and its impact on software development.
- Hands-On Tools: Work with tools like Git, Jenkins, Docker, and Kubernetes.
- Automation & Efficiency: Gain practical experience in automating the software delivery pipeline.
- Collaboration Focus: Enhance teamwork between development and operations teams throughout the SDLC.
